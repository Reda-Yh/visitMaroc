Our Story

Founded in 2024, our group emerged from a shared passion for education and innovation. Our mission is simple yet profound: to develop projects that enhance learning experiences and make education accessible to all.

Today, we are proud to be a collaborative team of students from École Polytechnique, dedicated to transforming ideas into impactful solutions. With a focus on creativity and teamwork, we strive to inspire and empower others through our work in the field of computer engineering.
