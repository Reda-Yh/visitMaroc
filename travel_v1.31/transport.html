The High-Speed Train (TGV) currently connects the cities of Tangier, Rabat, Casablanca, and Kenitra. With impressive speed, the journey between Tangier and Casablanca takes only 2 hours. You can purchase your tickets online or at ticket counters.

Urban Transport

Taxis

The small taxi is the most commonly used means of transport in Morocco. It operates only within the city and is identifiable by its color according to the city you are in. Red for Casablanca and Agadir, blue for Rabat, Tangier, and Asilah, and orange for Marrakech. If you return from an evening late or have an early morning flight to catch, you can book a "taxi on demand" or "green taxi" in most major cities.
